Perpermint Tart

prep time: 3 hrs 20 mins

Yield: 1

Ingredients




Steps

  1. Whip cream till thick
  2. Add caramel and mix well till combined.
  3. Stack the tortillas, 4 at a time, in a microwave oven and heat until warm and pliable, 20 to 30 seconds per batch.
  4. In a suitable dish (preferably square or rectangular) place a layer of biscuits - don't worry if there are small spaces in-between.
  5. Spoon a little bit of the caramel mixture over the biscuits to cover completely Grate a piece of the chocolate over caramel mixture.
  6. Repeat with layering biscuits, caramel and grated choscolate until finished, ending with a caramel & chocolate layer.
  7. Place in fridge to set for a few hours or overnight if you can
